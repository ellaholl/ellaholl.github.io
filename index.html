My story

I'm a software engineer who's a little too online — which is mostly how this blog happens. If something's trending and I can't stop thinking about it, I usually end up scraping the data behind it just to see what's actually going on. That curiosity carries over into everything else I do here too.

Outside of work and research projects, you'll usually find me with a paintbrush, a camera, or deep in a Wikipedia rabbit hole about something completely unrelated.
